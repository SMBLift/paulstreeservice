About Paul's Tree Service

A family tradition of exceptional tree care since 1987.

Our Story

Paul's Tree Service was founded in 1987 with a simple mission: provide honest, high-quality tree care to Pittsburgh homeowners at fair prices.

What started as a one-man operation has grown into one of Pittsburgh's most trusted tree service companies, now in its second generation of family ownership.

Paul Gregorius carries on the tradition his father started, combining decades of experience with modern equipment and techniques. Every job, big or small, gets the same attention to detail and respect for your property.

As a locally owned and operated business, we know Pittsburgh's trees, neighborhoods, and weather challenges better than anyone. We're not just your tree service — we're your neighbors.
